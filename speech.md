# NCHDS Bangladesh: The Centralized Healthcare System (CHCS) Elevator Pitch

**Target Duration**: 90 Seconds (~220 Words)  
**Goal**: Secure funding from the Ministry of Health, ICT Division, or international development partners.

---

### The Pitch

"Honorable partners, today in Bangladesh, when a patient travels from Mymensingh Medical to Rajshahi Medical, their clinical history is entirely lost. This systemic blindness forces patients to repeat identical diagnostic tests, costing families over **62 million dollars annually** in waste, while permitting counterfeit drugs to circulate completely undetected in our pharmacies. 

We have built the **National Centralized Healthcare Database System (NCHDS)** to solve this once and for all. Using a secure, high-concurrency microservices architecture capable of handling **50,000 requests per second**, we link every citizen to a unique **National Health ID (NHID)** derived cryptographically from their NID or Birth Certificate. 

Our platform connects the entire ecosystem:
1. **The Ministry** monitors live birth/death tickers and dynamic geographical disease clustering to contain outbreaks before they spread.
2. **Hospitals and Doctors** access longitudinal patient records and operate under an automated regulatory grading system.
3. **Pharmacies** scan barcode prescriptions, auto-syncing stock and blocking unauthorized drug sales.
4. **Patients** manage their records, log malpractice, and use an AI Personal Nurse to stay healthy.

This is not just a proposal; our live prototype is running today. By integrating this system, we will save millions of lives, eliminate financial leakages, and digitize universal health coverage for **175 million citizens**. 

We are seeking the partnership and funding to scale this pilot nationwide. Join us in building the digital spine of Bangladesh's healthcare future. Thank you."
